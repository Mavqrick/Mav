	<p style="font-size:18px">Lenovo Legion 7i, Legion 5Pi, and Legion 5i laptops have been launched in India.

	<h3>Advanced Coldfront Cooling</h3>
	<p>The Legion 5i 15 keeps its cool no matter how hard you push it with the Coldfront 2.0 system which cools and dissipates heat via a dual-channel thermal mechanism of 67 liquid crystal polymer-coated fan blades and copper pipes. 
	This means your laptop will run optimally, so you can always be at the top of your game. 
	And with Q Control 3.0 allowing you to manually select between Quiet, Balance, and Performance thermal modes, you’ll blast through noobs like a pro. Also, every Legion laptop features Dual Burn Support, pushing the CPU & GPU together for max performance in-game. 
	This combination of performance and cooling means no crashing, just owning.</p>
